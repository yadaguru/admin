Internal name for timeframe Possible timeframe types:
  • Absolute - Reminder will be grouped/sent on a specific date.
  • Relative - Reminder will be grouped/sent X days before the application due date
  • Today - Reminder will always display in the 'Today' group. NOTE: reminders with this timeframe will not be sent to the user.
Enter a number X days before the due date. Select an absolute date for the timeframe.
